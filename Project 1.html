Understanding the World

Topic: Colours of the Rainbow

Introduction

Today we will be learning about all the colours of the rainbow. Have you seen a rainbow before?

This is a rainbow. Look at all its beautiful colours.

Write your name in the box below and we'll get started!

Lesson 1

A rainbow has seven colours.

Can you guess which colours they are?

Lesson 2

The first colour of the rainbow is red.

Just like on a stop sign or strawberries.

Some say red is the colour of danger.

Lesson 3

The second colour of the rainbow is orange.

Just like orange juice or pumpkins.

Orange is the colour you get from mixing red with yellow.

Lesson 4

Can you guess what the third colour of the rainbow is?

Yes, you got it right! It's yellow.

Just like sunflowers and the sun.

Hint

Lesson 5

The fourth colour of the rainbow is green.

Green - just like kiwi and grass.

If you mix yellow and blue together, you get green!

Lesson 6

The fifth colour of the rainbow is blue.

Blue - just like the sky and the sea.

There are many many shades of blue and they all have different names.

Lesson 7

The sixth colour of the rainbow is indigo.

Indigo is a dark purplish blue.

The colour of blueberries are close to indigo.

Lesson 8

The seventh colour of the rainbow is violet.

Flowers called violets are violet in colour!

Violet is kind of like purple.

Summary

So there you have it!

The seven colours of the rainbow are:

  1. Red
  2. Orange
  3. Yellow
  4. Green
  5. Blue
  6. Indigo and
  7. Violet
